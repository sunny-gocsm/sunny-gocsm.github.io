Tier 0 of the Insight Hierarchy — the AI hero. One bold, plain-language sentence that answers "what + what to do"; the time-poor operator reads this and leaves. Calm indigo AI surface; tone tints the left edge only.

```jsx
<Verdict tone="risk" score={48} band="watch"
  stamp={<span className="stamp down">Down 12</span>}
  actions={<button className="btn btn-ai btn-sm">Open brief</button>}>
  Tailored Wellbeing is <span className="em">stalling</span> — appointments dropped to
  zero 6 days ago and setup is incomplete.
</Verdict>
```

Keep the sentence declarative and concrete (facts) or hedged (projections), matched to confidence. Never flood the card with tone color — it's a 4px left edge. Pair below with `WhyCard` drivers (Tier 1).
